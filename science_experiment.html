Purification of Water Experiment

Aim

To demonstrate a simple method for purifying water, emphasizing the importance of clean water in daily life, including ritual ablutions (Wudu).

Description

This experiment shows how sand filtration and boiling can remove impurities and make water safe for consumption and religious practices.

Observation

After passing water through layers of sand and charcoal, the water appears clearer. Boiling further eliminates any microorganisms, making it safe and clean.

Results

The experiment successfully demonstrates that water purification is essential for health and religious practices. Clean water is vital for prayers and hygiene.
