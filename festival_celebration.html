Eid-ul-Fitr

"Celebrating Faith, Family, and Festivity"

Eid-ul-Fitr marks the end of Ramadan, the holy month of fasting. It is a time for gratitude, joy, and reflection on the blessings received from Allah.

The festival is celebrated with special prayers at the mosque, giving Zakat al-Fitr to the needy, and sharing meals with family and friends. Traditional sweets like Sheer Khurma are prepared, and homes are decorated beautifully.

"Eid is not just a festival; it is a celebration of gratitude and togetherness."
