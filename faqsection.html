FAQ Section

Q1: What are the prayer timings at the college?

The college provides designated prayer breaks according to the five daily Salah times. Students are encouraged to perform prayers on time.

Q2: Are halal food options available in the cafeteria?

Yes, all meals served in the cafeteria are halal-certified and prepared according to Islamic dietary guidelines.

Q3: Can non-Muslim students join Islamic studies classes?

Non-Muslim students are welcome to attend optional Islamic studies sessions to learn about Islamic culture and history.

Q4: Is hijab mandatory for female students?

Female students are encouraged to wear the hijab on campus as part of respecting Islamic teachings, though optional for non-Muslim students.
