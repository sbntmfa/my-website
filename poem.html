Guided by Faith

By Amina Siddiqui

In the light of Allah, we find our way,
Through trials and nights, and the brightest day.
With prayers on lips and hearts so true,
We walk the path that is blessed for you.

rough patience and charity,our spirits rise,
Seeing the world through compassionate eyes.
Faith guides our steps, both near and far,
With hope and love as our guiding star.

Every dawn brings a new chance to grow,
To follow the teachings we truly know.
In submission to Allah, our hearts take flight,
Shining with grace and divine light.
