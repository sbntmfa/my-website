Al-Khair Foundation

"Serving Humanity, Guided by Faith"

Al-Khair Foundation is a Muslim NGO dedicated to providing education, healthcare, and relief aid to underprivileged communities around the world.

We focus on sustainable programs that help people achieve self-sufficiency while upholding Islamic values such as compassion, honesty, and justice.

"The best of people are those who benefit others." - Prophet Muhammad (PBUH)
