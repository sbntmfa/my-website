The Life of Prophet Muhammad (PBUH)

Written by: Dr. Amina Siddiqui, 2021

This book provides a detailed account of Prophet Muhammad's life, teachings, and his role in shaping Islamic civilization. It explores key events such as the Hijrah and the establishment of the Muslim community in Madinah.

The narrative emphasizes compassion, patience, and wisdom in his personal and public life, inspiring readers to follow his exemplary character.

The book also highlights major companions and their contributions, providing lessons applicable to modern life. Faith, perseverance, and moral guidance are central themes.

"The best among you are those who have the best character." - Prophet Muhammad (PBUH)
