Islamic Youth Leadership Seminar 2025

"Empowering Tomorrow's Muslim Leaders"

Date and Venue

Date: 15th December 2025

Venue: Al-Huda Community Center, Karachi, Pakistan

The seminar is designed to inspire young Muslims to develop leadership skills while staying rooted in Islamic principles. Speakers include scholars, educators, and community leaders who will share their knowledge and experiences.

Topics covered include community service, ethical decision-making, and personal development guided by Islamic values.

"Leadership is service, guided by faith and integrity."
