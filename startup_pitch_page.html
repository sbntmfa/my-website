HalalEats

"Connecting Muslims with Trusted Halal Food"

Problem Statement: Many Muslims struggle to find reliable halal food options in urban areas, leading to uncertainty about dietary compliance.

Solution: HalalEats is an app that verifies restaurants and products, ensuring halal-certified meals are easily accessible. Users can browse, rate, and order with confidence.

Mission: To create a global platform that empowers Muslims to maintain their dietary values while enjoying convenience and quality.
